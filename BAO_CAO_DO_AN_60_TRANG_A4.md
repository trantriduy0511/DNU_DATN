# BAO CAO DO AN TOT NGHIEP (DINH HUONG TOI THIEU 60 TRANG A4)

## 1) Thong tin chung de tai

- **Ten de tai**: Xay dung mang xa hoi nho cho cong dong DNU chia se thong tin hoc tap.
- **Don vi**: Khoa CNTT - Dai hoc Da Nang.
- **Sinh vien thuc hien**: Tran Tri Duy.
- **Giang vien huong dan**: TS. Tran Quy Nam.
- **Muc tieu tong quat**:
  - Xay dung he thong web ho tro sinh vien/giang vien chia se bai viet, tai lieu, su kien hoc tap.
  - Cung cap cac tinh nang mang xa hoi cot loi phuc vu boi canh hoc duong.
  - Dam bao bao mat, phan quyen, kha nang mo rong va trai nghiem nguoi dung.

---

## 2) Huong dan bien soan de dat toi thieu 60 trang A4

### 2.1. Tieu chuan dinh dang de nghi

- Kho giay A4, can le trai 3.5 cm, phai 2 cm, tren 2.5 cm, duoi 2.5 cm.
- Font Times New Roman, co chu 13 hoac 14.
- Gian dong 1.3 - 1.5.
- Danh so trang tu phan mo dau den het tai lieu.
- Chuong, muc, tieu muc danh so ro rang (1, 1.1, 1.1.1...).

### 2.2. Phan bo so trang de nghi (tong 60-75 trang)

- **Trang bia, nhan xet, loi cam on, muc luc, danh muc hinh/bang**: 8-12 trang.
- **Chuong 1 - Tong quan de tai**: 8-10 trang.
- **Chuong 2 - Co so ly thuyet va cong nghe**: 10-12 trang.
- **Chuong 3 - Phan tich va thiet ke he thong**: 12-15 trang.
- **Chuong 4 - Trien khai va ket qua thuc hien**: 16-20 trang.
- **Chuong 5 - Kiem thu, danh gia, huong phat trien**: 6-8 trang.
- **Tai lieu tham khao + Phu luc**: 6-10 trang.

---

## 3) De cuong bao cao chi tiet (co the dung lam muc luc chinh)

## CHUONG 1. TONG QUAN DE TAI (8-10 trang)

### 1.1. Ly do chon de tai

- Nhu cau ket noi hoc tap trong cong dong sinh vien DNU.
- Han che cua cac kenh trao doi thong tin roi rac.
- Nhu cau co nen tang noi bo, tap trung vao hoc thuat.

### 1.2. Muc tieu nghien cuu

- Xay dung he thong mang xa hoi hoc tap hoat dong on dinh.
- Ho tro trao doi kien thuc, tai lieu, su kien, nhom hoc tap.
- Cung cap dashboard quan tri va co che kiem soat noi dung.

### 1.3. Doi tuong, pham vi nghien cuu

- Doi tuong: sinh vien, giang vien, quan tri vien.
- Pham vi:
  - Chuc nang co ban cua mang xa hoi hoc tap.
  - Khong di sau thuong mai dien tu, thanh toan, quang cao.

### 1.4. Phuong phap thuc hien

- Thu thap yeu cau.
- Thiet ke kien truc.
- Phat trien backend/frontend.
- Kiem thu, toi uu, tai lieu hoa.

### 1.5. Cau truc bao cao

- Gioi thieu ngan 5 chuong va phan phu luc.

---

## CHUONG 2. CO SO LY THUYET VA CONG NGHE (10-12 trang)

### 2.1. Tong quan mo hinh Client-Server

- Nguyen ly hoat dong.
- Uu diem va han che.

### 2.2. Cong nghe backend

- Node.js, Express.js.
- MongoDB + Mongoose.
- JWT, bcryptjs, express-validator.
- Socket.io cho giao tiep real-time.

### 2.3. Cong nghe frontend

- React + Vite.
- React Router DOM.
- Zustand quan ly state.
- Tailwind CSS, Recharts, Axios.

### 2.4. Bao mat ung dung web

- Xac thuc va phan quyen.
- Hash mat khau.
- Kiem tra du lieu dau vao.
- CORS va cac bien phap phong ngua tan cong co ban.

### 2.5. Co so du lieu NoSQL va ly do lua chon MongoDB

- So sanh ngan gon SQL va NoSQL.
- Tinh linh hoat schema.
- Kha nang phat trien nhanh theo module.

---

## CHUONG 3. PHAN TICH VA THIET KE HE THONG (12-15 trang)

### 3.1. Khao sat yeu cau

#### 3.1.1. Yeu cau chuc nang

- Dang ky, dang nhap, quan ly ho so.
- Bai viet, binh luan, like, luu bai.
- Nhom hoc tap, su kien.
- Tim kiem, thong bao, ket ban.
- Quan tri user/noi dung/thong ke.

#### 3.1.2. Yeu cau phi chuc nang

- Hieu nang, bao mat, kha nang su dung, kha nang mo rong.

### 3.2. Phan tich tac nhan va use case

- Tac nhan: User, Admin.
- Use case tong quan.
- Mo ta 3-5 use case quan trong theo mau:
  - Ten use case.
  - Dieu kien bat dau.
  - Luong chinh.
  - Luong ngoai le.
  - Ket qua.

### 3.3. Thiet ke kien truc tong the

- So do kien truc:
  - Frontend (React).
  - Backend API (Express).
  - Database (MongoDB).
  - Realtime layer (Socket.io).
- Mo ta luong du lieu tu giao dien den CSDL.

### 3.4. Thiet ke co so du lieu

- Cac collection chinh:
  - User, Post, Comment, Group, Event, Message, Notification, Report.
- Trinh bay:
  - Truong du lieu quan trong.
  - Quan he tham chieu ObjectId.
  - Index de tang toc tim kiem.

### 3.5. Thiet ke API

- Nguyen tac RESTful.
- Quy uoc status code.
- Cau truc JSON response.
- Danh sach endpoint theo module:
  - Auth, User, Post, Comment, Group, Event, Friend, Admin.

### 3.6. Thiet ke giao dien

- Wireframe:
  - Trang dang nhap/dang ky.
  - Trang chu user.
  - Trang admin dashboard.
- Nguyen tac UX:
  - De su dung, thong nhat, responsive.

---

## CHUONG 4. TRIEN KHAI HE THONG VA NOI DUNG DA THUC HIEN (16-20 trang)

> Day la chuong trong tam de trinh bay "nhung gi da lam" va "ket qua thuc te".

### 4.1. Moi truong phat trien

- He dieu hanh, Node.js, npm.
- MongoDB local/cloud.
- Cong cu: VS Code/Cursor, Postman.

### 4.2. To chuc source code

- Cau truc thu muc:
  - `backend/` (routes, controllers, models, middleware).
  - `frontend/` (pages, components, store, utils).
- Nguyen tac tach module.

### 4.3. Trien khai module xac thuc (Auth)

- Chuc nang da lam:
  - Dang ky, dang nhap, dang xuat.
  - Lay thong tin nguoi dung hien tai.
  - Doi mat khau, cap nhat profile.
- Luong xu ly:
  - Hash mat khau bang bcrypt.
  - Tao JWT khi dang nhap thanh cong.
  - Middleware xac thuc token.
- Minh chung de dua vao bao cao:
  - Anh man hinh trang dang nhap/dang ky.
  - Anh test API login/register.

### 4.4. Trien khai module bai viet va tuong tac (Post + Comment)

- Chuc nang da lam:
  - Tao/sua/xoa bai viet.
  - Xem danh sach bai viet.
  - Like/unlike, luu bai.
  - Them/sua/xoa binh luan.
- Diem ky thuat:
  - Phan trang va sap xep bai viet.
  - Populate thong tin tac gia, thong tin binh luan.
- Minh chung:
  - Anh giao dien news feed.
  - Anh ket qua tao bai viet va like/binh luan.

### 4.5. Trien khai module nhom hoc tap (Group)

- Chuc nang da lam:
  - Tao nhom, tham gia, roi nhom.
  - Hien thi danh sach nhom.
  - Quan ly thanh vien (neu da co).
- Minh chung:
  - Anh giao dien danh sach nhom.
  - Anh API tham gia/roi nhom.

### 4.6. Trien khai module su kien (Event)

- Chuc nang da lam:
  - Tao su kien, cap nhat su kien.
  - Dang ky tham gia su kien.
  - Hien thi su kien sap toi.
- Minh chung:
  - Anh danh sach su kien.
  - Anh API tao su kien/ join event.

### 4.7. Trien khai module ket ban (Friend)

- Chuc nang da lam:
  - Gui loi moi ket ban.
  - Chap nhan/tu choi loi moi.
  - Danh sach ban be.
- Minh chung:
  - Anh UI ket ban.
  - Anh API friend request.

### 4.8. Trien khai dashboard quan tri (Admin)

- Chuc nang da lam:
  - Dashboard thong ke tong quan.
  - Quan ly user (xem/khoa/xoa).
  - Quan ly bai viet (duyet/xoa).
- Bieu do:
  - Thong ke user theo thang.
  - Thong ke bai viet theo danh muc.
- Minh chung:
  - Anh giao dien dashboard.
  - Anh bang quan ly user va bai viet.

### 4.9. Trien khai tinh nang real-time (neu da hoan thien)

- Noi dung:
  - Chat real-time.
  - Thong bao real-time.
- Mo ta su kien Socket:
  - Ket noi.
  - Gui/nhan tin nhan.
  - Ngat ket noi.
- Minh chung:
  - Anh hai trinh duyet chat thoi gian thuc.

### 4.10. Trien khai upload file va quan ly media (neu da co)

- Upload tai lieu/anh cho bai viet.
- Gioi han dung luong, loai file.
- Xu ly loi upload.

### 4.11. Trien khai tim kiem

- Tim kiem bai viet theo tu khoa.
- Tim kiem user/nhom/su kien.
- Co che loc va sap xep ket qua.

### 4.12. Trien khai bao mat

- Hash mat khau.
- Kiem soat quyen truy cap.
- Validation dau vao.
- Xu ly loi tap trung.

### 4.13. Cac van de gap phai va cach xu ly

- Van de dong bo du lieu giua frontend/backend.
- Van de CORS/token het han.
- Van de truy van cham.
- Cach khac phuc da ap dung.

## 6) Mau viet phan "Ket qua dat duoc"

**Doan van mau 1**  
He thong da xay dung thanh cong nen tang mang xa hoi hoc tap cho cong dong DNU voi cac chuc nang cot loi nhu dang ky/dang nhap, quan ly bai viet, binh luan, ket ban, nhom hoc tap va su kien. Nguoi dung co the de dang tuong tac, chia se thong tin hoc tap, qua do nang cao hieu qua ket noi va hoc tap trong nha truong.

**Doan van mau 2**  
Ve mat ky thuat, de tai da ap dung kien truc client-server voi frontend React va backend Node.js/Express, su dung MongoDB de luu tru du lieu. Cac co che bao mat co ban gom hash mat khau, xac thuc JWT, kiem tra du lieu dau vao da duoc trien khai. Dashboard quan tri giup theo doi tinh hinh van hanh, ho tro kiem soat noi dung va nguoi dung.

**Doan van mau 3**  
Qua qua trinh phat trien, nhom de tai da giai quyet duoc nhieu van de thuc te lien quan den dong bo du lieu, to chuc module, va trai nghiem nguoi dung. Ket qua dat duoc cho thay he thong dap ung muc tieu de ra, dong thoi tao nen tang cho cac huong nang cap nhu toi uu hieu nang, bo sung AI goi y, va trien khai cloud trong giai doan tiep theo.
